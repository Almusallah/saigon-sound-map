About Saigon Sound Map

Saigon Sound Map is a collaborative project initiated by Officine GAP, an interdisciplinary creative studio based in Ho Chi Minh City.

The project invites the community to record, upload, and share the everyday sounds of Ho Chi Minh City and South Vietnam — from street vendors and motorbike traffic to temple bells, market chatter, and monsoon rains.

Our aim is to build a living sound ecology repository — a sonic archive that preserves the rich acoustic identity of this vibrant region for researchers, artists, and anyone curious about the soundscape of everyday life in Saigon.
