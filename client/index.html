Welcome to the Saigon_Miền Tây Sound Map

This project explores everyday urban life through its acoustic environment.

The map invites slow listening and attention to the changing sonic environments of the region.

This is an open and evolving archive — listen and contribute respectfully. When recording in public or semi-public environments, please be mindful of the surrounding context, environments, and forms of life connected to these spaces and sounds.

About Saigon_Miền Tây Sound Map

The Saigon_Miền Tây Sound Map is a collaborative project initiated by Officine Gặp, an interdisciplinary creative studio based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

The project invites the community to record and share the everyday sounds of Ho Chi Minh City and the southern provinces — from street vendors and motorbike traffic to everyday rituals and ceremonies, market chatter, and monsoon rains.

Together, these recordings form a growing, living sound repository: a resource for artists, researchers, and anyone interested in listening more closely to the rhythms and textures of life in southern Vietnam.

This map is evolving — feedback, ideas, and suggestions are always welcome.
